About

Hornclaw is our humble attempt to ‘fix’ the glaring and longstanding asymmetries rampant in traditional stock markets - information gaps and opacity, lack of expertise, and many other such realities - which effectively skew success in favour of the wealthy and act as entry barriers for the ‘masses’. Signs that a socio-fiscal revolution is brewing have been apparent ever since cryptocurrencies hit the common consciousness of the young and hungry across the globe. The record smashing numbers, the sheer scale of adoption, the passionate conversations on social media and in living rooms - all pointed to the fact that the world is waiting to disrupt finance as we’ve known it for the last few centuries. The aim that we seek to achieve with Hornclaw is to bring the worlds of trading and gaming together in ways that are easy to understand and risk-efficient. Hornclaw - for the people, in the language of the people, and in favour of the people.

Our Vision

Enabling wealth creation for the lowest common denominator by capitalising on the immense power of sports viewership and fan enthusiasm with blockchain based gaming solutions which are designed on the most basic principles of capital markets and trading; and to be able to do so in a democratic manner.

Mission

To build tech gaming solutions that enable:

  • Users to employ their sports knowledge to build wealth by trading in the stocks of sportspersons on our flagship applications
  • Users to have real ownership of assets with smart tokens
  • Streamlined, secure and transparent transactions
  • A community of users who can contribute and help grow the ecosystem
